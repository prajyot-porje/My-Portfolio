# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters, engineers, engineering managers, CTOs, and founders at top Indian and global tech companies. They are looking for highly capable full-stack/AI/ML engineers who can deliver production-grade products, think strategically, and execute under real business constraints without hand-holding.

## Product Purpose
A personal portfolio presenting Prajyot Porje as a Product Engineer of exceptional craft and entrepreneurial proof. It exists to establish the identity: "Prajyot doesn't wait for permission to build real things. He already has." Success means immediate comprehension of his track record, professional competence, and attention to detail.

## Brand Personality
Direct, specific, technically precise, and authoritative. 
- **Voice/Tone**: Facts over adjectives. First-person, present tense. Straightforward and clean.
- **3-Word Personality**: Direct, Technical, Premium.
- **Emotional Goals**: Evokes expert confidence, reliability, and visual/functional precision.

## Anti-references
- Typical SaaS templates using gradient text, glassmorphism, or frosted glass cards.
- Side-stripe borders, gradient borders, and spotlight/cursor glow effects.
- Typing or rotating text animations in the hero.
- Skill percentage bars, progress indicators, or logo-heavy accordions.
- Preloaders or generic canvas/particle backgrounds.
- Any copy that is vague, self-aggrandizing, or lacks verifiable facts.

## Design Principles
- **Practice What You Preach**: Every statistic, metric, and case study detail must be real, verifiable, and backed by functional code.
- **Show, Don't Tell**: Avoid empty adjectives like "passionate" or "dynamic." Let the architectural decisions and delivered results speak for themselves.
- **Visual & Technical Precision**: The level of craft must meet the bar of Vercel, Stripe, and Linear. No sloppy alignments, half-measures, or layout overflows.
- **Expert Confidence**: Communicate with absolute technical clarity (e.g., using terms like "multi-tenant context architecture" rather than general marketing buzzwords).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **WCAG AA Compliance**: All text elements must maintain a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 against their backgrounds (no low-contrast grey-on-cream text).
- **Reduced Motion**: Mandatory `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` overrides for all visual transitions and animations.
- **Progressive Enhancement**: The site must be fully usable and readable without JavaScript.
- **Responsive Layout**: Pixel-perfect scaling from 375px (mobile) to 1440px (desktop) without container overflow.
